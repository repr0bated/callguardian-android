# CallGuardian Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## Introduction

CallGuardian ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our CallGuardian mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Call and Message Data
We collect the following information to provide call screening and spam protection services:

- **Call Logs**: Incoming and outgoing call information including phone numbers, call duration, and call timestamps
- **SMS/MMS Messages**: Message content, sender information, and message timestamps
- **Call Metadata**: Call type (incoming, outgoing, missed), call status, and interaction outcomes

### 2. Contact Information
To enhance our lookup and blocking capabilities:

- **Contact List**: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses from your device contacts
- **Contact Photos**: Profile pictures associated with contacts (optional)

### 3. Device and Usage Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **App Usage**: Feature usage patterns, app performance data, and interaction logs

### 4. Third-Party API Data
We use the following third-party services for phone number lookup:

- **NumLookup API**: For carrier and location information
- **Abstract API**: For phone validation and carrier details
- **Hugging Face API**: For AI-powered spam risk assessment (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**: Screen calls, identify spam, and block unwanted communications
2. **Enhance Protection**: Analyze patterns to improve spam detection accuracy
3. **Personalize Experience**: Customize blocking rules and notification preferences
4. **Technical Support**: Diagnose and resolve app issues
5. **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 1. Third-Party Service Providers
- **API Providers**: NumLookup, Abstract API, and Hugging Face for phone number lookup and AI analysis
- **Analytics Services**: For app performance and usage analysis

### 2. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

### 3. Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to authorized personnel only
- **Secure Storage**: Local storage with optional encryption for sensitive data
- **Regular Audits**: Periodic security assessments and updates

## Your Rights and Choices

### 1. Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access and review your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of certain data processing activities

### 2. App Permissions
You can control app permissions through your device settings:
- **Call Log Access**: Required for call screening functionality
- **SMS Access**: Required for message screening
- **Contact Access**: Optional, enhances lookup accuracy
- **Phone State**: Required for call handling

### 3. Data Deletion
To request complete data deletion:
1. Open the App settings
2. Navigate to "Privacy" section
3. Select "Delete All Data"
4. Confirm your request

## Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Typically:

- Call and message logs: 90 days
- Contact information: Until you uninstall the app or delete it manually
- Lookup cache: 30 days
- Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, where data protection laws may differ. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
