### Sensitive Permissions

**Q: Does the app use sensitive permissions?**
> Yes

**Sensitive permissions used:**
- READ_CALL_LOG - Required for call screening functionality
- READ_PHONE_STATE - Required for call handling
- READ_SMS/RECEIVE_SMS - Required for message filtering
- READ_CONTACTS - Optional, for trusted number identification
- ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS - Required for call blocking

**Q: Are permissions necessary for core functionality?**
> Yes (all sensitive permissions are required for the app's core purpose)

## Additional Disclosures

### App Purpose Statement
CallGuardian is a utility app designed to protect users from spam calls and unwanted messages. It uses AI-powered analysis and reverse phone lookup services to identify and block potentially harmful communications.

### Age Requirement
While the content is appropriate for all ages, the app requires certain permissions that may be subject to parental controls on devices used by minors.

### Privacy Compliance

- CCPA compliant
- No data collection from children under 13
