## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Tired of spam calls and unwanted messages? CallGuardian is your intelligent shield against telemarketers, scammers, and robocalls. With advanced AI-powered analysis and real-time reverse lookup, CallGuardian gives you complete control over who can reach you.

PROACTIVE PROTECTION
• Block spam calls before they ring
• Filter unwanted SMS and MMS messages
• AI-powered risk assessment for every interaction
• Real-time caller identification and lookup

INTELLIGENT ANALYSIS
• Reverse phone lookup with multiple data sources
• AI-driven spam probability scoring
• Smart contact integration for trusted numbers
• Detailed call and message history

TOTAL CONTROL
• Customizable block modes (calls, messages, or both)
• One-tap block/allow actions from notifications
• Save unknown numbers to contacts instantly
• Granular permission controls

PRIVACY FIRST
• All data stored locally on your device
• No cloud storage of your call logs or messages
• Encrypted preferences and settings
• Transparent data usage

KEY FEATURES
• Call screening with instant lookup
• SMS filtering and spam detection
• AI-powered risk assessment
• Multiple reverse lookup sources
• Custom API endpoint support
• Contact sync and management
• Beautiful Material Design interface
• Battery-efficient background operation

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 10 or later
• Call screening permission for call protection
• Default SMS app permission for message filtering
• Internet connection for lookup services

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
• Call Log Access: Required to analyze and screen incoming calls
• SMS Access: Required to filter and analyze messages
• Phone State: Required for call handling and blocking
• Contacts: Optional - enhances lookup accuracy
• Notifications: Required to show lookup results

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
CallGuardian integrates with free lookup APIs:
• NumLookup API for carrier information
• Abstract API for phone validation
• Hugging Face AI for spam detection (optional)

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
We believe in privacy by design. CallGuardian stores all sensitive data locally on your device, never uploads your call logs or messages to the cloud, and provides easy data deletion options.

PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZED
• Lightweight and battery-efficient
• Smart caching for faster lookups
• Background operation with minimal impact
• Optimized for all Android devices

PERFECT FOR
• Anyone tired of spam calls
• Users wanting control over communications
• Privacy-conscious individuals
• Small business owners
• Seniors needing scam protection

Download CallGuardian today and take back control of your phone!
```
